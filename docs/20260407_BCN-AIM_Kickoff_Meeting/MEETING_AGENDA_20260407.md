## Agenda (30 min)

### 1. Welcome & Context (5 min)
- Brief intro of the FedClinAI initiative
- Why BCN-AIM Lab and fedder.ai were selected
- EIC Pathfinder Open deadline: **May 12, 2026**

### 2. Concept Overview (10 min)
- Core idea: federated learning for clinical AI without raw data transfer
- Privacy stack: differential privacy (ε=2.0/round, δ=10⁻⁵), SecAgg+, OMOP normalization
- Contribution scoring: Federated Shapley Value
- MVP: T2DM hospitalization prediction across 3–5 Georgian clinics (AUC >0.75)
- Budget: €2,275,000 (EIC ceiling €4M)
