### Key Narrative Points (from CONCEPT.md)

1. **Challenge:** Clinical AI is siloed; patient data cannot leave institutions (GDPR/HIPAA)
2. **Approach:** Privacy-preserving FL with 5-layer privacy stack (de-identification → k-anonymity → DP-SGD → Krum → SecAgg+)
3. **Innovation:** Rényi DP extends training from 5 rounds (linear) to ~30–40 rounds; Shapley scoring enables fair data contribution incentives
4. **Impact:** Cross-clinic prediction of adverse outcomes (hospitalization ≥12m) without raw data sharing
